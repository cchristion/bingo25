How to Play ? (Click to Open)
  1. Start: Everyone opens the game on their own phone to get a unique board.
  2. Play: Take turns shouting a number.
    • Player A shouts: "I pick 7!"
    • Everyone finds 7 and clicks it.
    • Player B shouts the next number.
  3. Win: The first person to get 5 lines and see "BINGO" wins!
